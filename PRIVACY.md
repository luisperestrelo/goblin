# Privacy Policy

This is a personal, non-commercial application. Its sole user is the
application owner. It retrieves the owner's own bank account information
(balances and transactions) via the Enable Banking API, with the owner's
explicit consent given through their bank's authentication.

Data is stored exclusively on the owner's personal device. No data is
shared with, sold to, or made accessible to any third party.
